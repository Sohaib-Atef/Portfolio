The Analyst Behind Numbers

After graduating with a degree in Management Information Systems, my curiosity led me to explore computer networks and how data moves across systems.

During that time, I worked in different roles — from customer service to content management — at international companies serving multiple markets.

While working closely with managers, I was fascinated by how they constantly refined KPIs and transformed massive amounts of raw data into simple, clear dashboards that supported better decisions.

That was the moment I realized my real passion lies in data analysis, organization, and visualization.

Today, I hold a certification in Data Analysis with Power BI and Excel through the Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative, and I'm excited to keep growing in this field and help businesses make smarter, data-driven decisions.
